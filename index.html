MYSORE DARSHINI
At last the classic food from the Royal city of Mysore has arrived in Canada
After successfully completing 25 years career in the software industry, Shailu the executive chef and CEO of Mysore Darshini is pursuing his lifelong dream and passion of serving simple soul food to his guests. His motto is "No confusion through fusion, prepare fresh food in its simple and original form, by following his grandma's and mom's recipes”

Our Best
Classic Dishes
Mysore Darshini takes pride in serving classical dishes from the homes of south India, these are simple dishes, yet its hard to find the authentic ones in Canada ! Like Tomato Rasam, Masala Vada, Home style Masala Dosa, Chicken 65, Masala rice Roti, puloas and mysore pak and of course south Indian filter coffee.
Home Style Pulao
Rice cooked with vegetables and aromatic spices.
Vada
Mildly spiced lentil donuts with grated coconut and chillies.
Mysore Masala Dosa
Mysore Darshini takes pride in making this rice and lentil crepe stuffed with potato baaji in authentic home style. A bit thicker than normal, crispy on the outside and soft on the inside.
Mysore Pak
A sweet delicacy which created specially for the Maharaja of Mysore, made from lentil flour, sugar and ghee. (2 pieces per plate)
